André Tatarka

The fresh blood in our stock, young and talented singer 

Andre Tatarka was born on 15/8/2004. Andre is very talented teenager, just like the name of his first single, is a dream of many young girls.
 
He inherited all the best genes from his equally talented mother first Czechoslovakian beauty pageant runner up Ms. Andrea Tatarkova. From a young age, Andre was no stranger to the theatre and art scene
 
Music has always played important role in his life, as a three years old Andre could sing all the classic songs of Karel Gott, Elan and many others. With his mom he visited theatres and saw lot of musicals and that’s where he got most of his inspiration.
 
Young Andre is highly talented, he is good at kickboxing, skiing and dancing, Andre also does some modelling and it doesn’t hurt that he also got the looks.
 
Family is very important to Andre, he is very close to his younger brother Alex. Andre is a smart, kind boy who’s got respect for people around him, and he keeps traditional family values and loves his grandparents the most.
 
Andre’s first single was “Single Dream”. He also released a second one named “Duha” which means rainbow in Slovak, both singles are about first love experience.
Andre is becoming a house hold name among young adults.
