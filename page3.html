Filip Jančík

Filip is modern violinist and producer.
He has successfully performed in prestigious venues in more than 15 countries around the world including Kuwait, Sweden, Denmark, the USA, Russia, Singapore, Belgium or Austria. 

Among others, he has collaborated with many world class musicians, producers and film composers from all around the world such as Avril Lavigne, Chris Baseford (Nickelback) or "Synchron Stage" (world class Top 3 scoring orchestra) in Vienna, Austria.

Back in 2015, he has also performed as a solo player during the opening ceremony of the Special Olympics World Games in Los Angeles.
